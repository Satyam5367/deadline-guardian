⚡ About
Deadline Guardian is the first truly agentic productivity companion — powered by Gemini 1.5 Pro function calling. Unlike reminder apps, it runs a multi-step reasoning loop: analyzes your tasks, calls specialized tools, detects cascading conflicts, identifies your procrastination patterns, and autonomously builds rescue plans.
